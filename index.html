My projects üë©üèª‚Äçüíª

I am currently working as a freelance developer and web designer. Some of the most incredible projects I am working on right now include developing the webpage for Somatic Pilates from beginning to end. This project has presented some unique challenges, particularly when the vision was not feasible from a design perspective. From the outset, we collaborated on a Figma board where we clearly outlined areas for experimentation and creativity, as well as identifying where we faced limitations. This open dialogue helped set realistic expectations while still allowing room for creative exploration. The client is working with a very unique color palette, as she wants to convey both strength and sensibility to create a safe space within the website.
By the time the prototype was completed, it was already close to the final vision. I then developed the site using HTML, CSS (with Bootstrap), and JavaScript, and initially hosted it on Netlify to give the client a chance to interact with the site before moving it to their domain. I am particularly proud of this project because it not only represented the client's brand well but also aligned with a meaningful cause. Through patience and transparency, we were able to craft a space that was both visually engaging and functionally sound, resulting in a site that embodied the company's values while being user-friendly and accessible.

I have worked on many projects for educational companies, such as Instrucko, a company based in India that teaches languages to children and teenagers. My role there was to develop content for Spanish students, encompassing both visual elements and linguistic knowledge. The company has a very strict visual palette for each language, as well as for visuals in general, which did not allow for much creativity. However, I had more freedom when it came to designing individual activities for the students. This role taught me how to balance working on designs I believed in while maintaining the brand's look and meeting the client's needs. There is always a way to be creative.

These are some other projects (including educational, client, and personal projects). You will find almost everything on my portfolio, except for the deployment of projects that use the OpenAI API due to security reasons.
