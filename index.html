My projects

I am working as a freelance developer at the moment and working right now in three projects for very three different clients. As metioned in my video Somatic Pilates is one of the projects i am working, developing the webpage from beginning to end. This project has presented some unique challenges in particular when the vision was not feasible from a design point of view. From the outset, we collaborated on a Figma board where we clearly outlined areas where we could experiment and get creative, and where we faced limitations. This open dialogue helped set realistic expectations while still leaving room for creative exploration. This client is working with a very uniquete color palette since she wants to convey both strength and sensibility to create a safe space within the website
By the time the prototype was done, it was already close to the final vision. I then developed the site using HTML, CSS (with Bootstrap), and JavaScript, and initially hosted it on Netlify to give the client a chance to interact with the site before we moved it to their domain. I’m particularly proud of this project because it not only represented the client’s brand well but also aligned with a meaningful cause. Through patience and transparency, we were able to craft a space that was both visually engaging and functionally sound, resulting in a site that embodied the company’s values while being user-friendly and accessible.

I have worked in a lot of projects for educational companies like Instrucko a company based in India that was teaching languages to children and teenages. My role there was to develope the content for the Spanish students from the visuals to the linguistics knowledge. The company has a very strick visual palette for each language and also visuals in general which did not allow a lot of creativity, however I had free range when it came to the individual activities for the students This role thought me to find ways to work on designs I believed while maintaining the brand look and what the client wanted. There is always a way to be creative.
